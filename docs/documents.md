# Company Registration Documents - FundLab SL (Andorra)

This document lists all required documents to register **FundLab SL** as a *Sociedad Limitada* (SL) in Andorra. FundLab SL is a SaaS platform to help businesses find investment.

---

## **A. CORE BUSINESS DOCUMENTS**

### 1. Business Plan
*   **Purpose**: Comprehensive plan detailing FundLab's strategic value, business model, target market, revenue projections, and alignment with Andorra's economic priorities
*   **Requirements**: Must demonstrate viability and value to Andorran economy
*   **Language**: Spanish or Catalan (official Andorran language)

### 2. Company Statutes / Articles of Association (Estatuts Socials)
*   **Purpose**: Constitutive document outlining company rules and structure
*   **Must Include**:
    *   Company name: **FundLab SL** (or **FundLab Societat Limitada**)
    *   Registered office address in Andorra
    *   Corporate purpose: "SaaS platform to help businesses find investment"
    *   Share capital structure: Minimum €3,000
    *   Number and par value of shares
    *   Distribution of shares among shareholders
    *   Directors/administrators details
    *   Governance rules
*   **Status**: Draft version prepared by lawyer, finalized with notary

### 3. Business Model Documentation
*   **Purpose**: Detailed explanation of:
    *   How FundLab operates (matching algorithm, subscription model, etc.)
    *   Target customers (startups seeking investment)
    *   Revenue model (subscription tiers, pricing)
    *   Technology stack (Next.js, Supabase, Gemini AI)
    *   Competitive advantages
*   **Format**: Professional document or presentation deck

### 4. Financial Projections
*   **Purpose**: 3-5 year financial forecast
*   **Must Include**:
    *   Projected revenue (subscription income)
    *   Operating expenses (hosting, AI API costs, salaries)
    *   Break-even analysis
    *   Funding requirements
*   **Format**: Spreadsheet with supporting narrative

### 5. Terms of Service (ToS)
*   **Purpose**: Legal agreement between FundLab and users
*   **Must Cover**:
    *   Service description
    *   User obligations
    *   Data processing and privacy
    *   Payment terms
    *   Liability limitations
    *   Dispute resolution
*   **Compliance**: Must comply with GDPR and Andorran data protection laws

### 6. Privacy Policy
*   **Purpose**: Explains how FundLab collects, uses, stores, and protects user data
*   **Must Cover**:
    *   Data collected (emails, company info, search history)
    *   Legal basis for processing
    *   Data retention periods
    *   User rights (access, deletion, portability)
    *   Cookie policy
    *   International data transfers (if using US-based Supabase/Gemini)
*   **Compliance**: GDPR / Andorran Data Protection Agency (APDA)

### 7. Acceptable Use Policy
*   **Purpose**: Rules for appropriate use of the platform
*   **Must Cover**:
    *   Prohibited activities (spam, misuse of investor data)
    *   Account suspension/termination conditions
    *   Intellectual property rights

## **B. SHAREHOLDER/FOUNDER PERSONAL DOCUMENTS**

### 8. Passport Copy
*   **Requirements**: 
    *   Legalized or certified copy
    *   Hague Apostille if from country that signed the Hague Convention
    *   Valid and unexpired
*   **Quantity**: One per shareholder/director

### 9. Criminal Record Certificate (Certificado de Antecedentes Penales)
*   **Requirements**:
    *   Issued by country of residence/nationality
    *   Not older than **3 months**
    *   Legalized with **Hague Apostille**
    *   Notarized translation into **Spanish** if not in Spanish/Catalan
*   **Quantity**: One per shareholder/director

### 10. Proof of Professional Background (CV/Resume)
*   **Purpose**: Demonstrates expertise relevant to running FundLab
*   **Must Include**:
    *   Educational background
    *   Work experience (especially tech/SaaS/investment sectors)
    *   Relevant skills
*   **Format**: Professional CV in Spanish or English

### 11. Proof of Address
*   **Purpose**: Verification of current residence
*   **Acceptable Documents**:
    *   Utility bill (electricity, water, gas)
    *   Bank statement
    *   Rental contract
*   **Requirements**: Not older than 3 months
*   **Quantity**: One per shareholder/director

### 12. Tax Compliance Certificate (Certificado de Estar Al Corriente con Hacienda)
*   **Purpose**: Proof shareholder has no outstanding tax debts
*   **Issued By**: Tax authority of country of residence
*   **Requirements**: Recent (within 3-6 months)

### 13. Beneficial Owners Declaration (UBO Declaration)
*   **Purpose**: Identifies all Ultimate Beneficial Owners (persons owning >10%)
*   **Must Include**:
    *   Full name, nationality, DOB
    *   Residential address
    *   Percentage of ownership
    *   Nature of control (direct/indirect)
*   **Format**: Notarized declaration

## **C. COMPANY FORMATION & REGISTRATION DOCUMENTS**

### 14. Company Name Reservation Certificate
*   **Process**: Submit several name options to Andorran Register
*   **Requirement**: Name must include "SL" or "Societat Limitada"
*   **Suggested Names**:
    *   FundLab SL
    *   FundLab Societat Limitada
*   **Status**: Reserved name certificate issued by Register

### 15. Bank Account Opening Certificate
*   **Purpose**: Proof of opening Andorran bank account and depositing minimum capital
*   **Requirements**:
    *   Account opened in FundLab SL's name
    *   Minimum **€3,000** deposited (share capital)
    *   Bank certificate confirming deposit
*   **Timing**: Must be obtained **before** notarial incorporation

### 16. Proof of Funds Origin
*   **Purpose**: For bank account opening, explain source of €3,000 capital
*   **Acceptable Proof**:
    *   Bank statements showing savings
    *   Investment sale proceeds
    *   Gift letter (if funds from family/friends)
    *   Employment income records
*   **Requirements**: Clear audit trail

### 17. Registered Office Lease Agreement or Ownership Proof
*   **Purpose**: FundLab must have physical registered office in Andorra
*   **Options**:
    *   **Lease Contract**: Rental agreement for office space
    *   **Virtual Office Agreement**: Service provider offering registered office address
    *   **Ownership Deed**: If purchasing property
*   **Requirements**: Must include full Andorran address

### 18. Foreign Investment Authorization (if applicable)
*   **Requirement**: If any shareholder is non-Andorran and owns >10% of capital
*   **Process**: Apply to Andorran government (Ministry of Economy)
*   **Documents Required**:
    *   Passport copy
    *   Criminal record certificate
    *   Proof of legal origin of funds
    *   Business plan
    *   Justification for investment in Andorra
*   **Timeline**: Can take several weeks to months

### 19. Notarial Deed of Incorporation (Escriptura de Constitució)
*   **Process**: Articles of Association signed before Andorran notary
*   **Output**: Official deed of incorporation
*   **Action**: Notary registers company with Andorran Company Register
*   **Language**: Catalan or Spanish

### 20. Commercial Registration Certificate
*   **Purpose**: Proof company is registered in Andorran Company Register (Registre de Comerç)
*   **Issued By**: Andorran Company Register
*   **Obtained**: After notarial deed execution

### 21. Tax Registration Number (NRT - Número de Registre Tributari)
*   **Purpose**: Official tax identification number for FundLab SL
*   **Process**: Apply to Andorran Tax Authority (Departament de Tributs i Fronteres)
*   **Timing**: Post-incorporation mandatory step

### 22. Social Security Registration (CASS)
*   **Purpose**: Register company with Andorran social security system
*   **Requirements**:
    *   Company name: FundLab SL
    *   Registered address
    *   Tax identification number (NRT)
    *   Number of employees
*   **Agency**: Caixa Andorrana de Seguretat Social (CASS)

## **D. OPERATIONAL & COMPLIANCE DOCUMENTS (Pre/Post-Launch)**

### 23. Data Processing Agreement (DPA)
*   **Purpose**: Contractual agreement with third-party processors (Supabase, Google Gemini)
*   **Must Cover**:
    *   Scope of processing
    *   Security measures
    *   Sub-processor authorization
    *   Data breach notification procedures
*   **Compliance**: GDPR Article 28

### 24. Data Protection Impact Assessment (DPIA)
*   **Purpose**: Assess privacy risks of FundLab's data processing
*   **Required When**: High risk to user rights (AI-driven matching, investor data aggregation)
*   **Must Cover**:
    *   Data flows
    *   Necessity and proportionality
    *   Risks to data subjects
    *   Mitigation measures
*   **Compliance**: GDPR Article 35

### 25. Cookie Consent Mechanism Documentation
*   **Purpose**: Legal documentation of how FundLab obtains cookie consent
*   **Must Include**:
    *   Cookie banner design
    *   Consent management system
    *   Cookie categories (essential, analytics, marketing)
    *   Record of consent
*   **Compliance**: ePrivacy Directive / GDPR

### 26. Employee Contracts (if hiring)
*   **Purpose**: Employment agreements for any hired staff
*   **Must Include**:
    *   Job description
    *   Salary and benefits
    *   Working hours
    *   Termination clauses
*   **Compliance**: Andorran labor law

### 27. Licenses and Permits (if applicable)
*   **Status**: Check if SaaS/fintech platform requires specific licenses in Andorra
*   **Potential Requirements**:
    *   If handling payments: Financial services license
    *   If storing sensitive data: Data protection registration with APDA
*   **Action**: Consult Andorran regulatory advisor

### 28. Intellectual Property Documentation
*   **Purpose**: Protect FundLab brand and technology
*   **Recommended**:
    *   **Trademark Registration**: Register "FundLab" with Andorran IP Office
    *   **Copyright**: Document code ownership (proprietary codebase)
    *   **Domain Registration**: Secure fundlab.ad or relevant domains
*   **Optional**: Software patents (if unique algorithm is patentable)

### 29. Shareholder Agreement
*   **Purpose**: Governs relationship between shareholders (if multiple)
*   **Must Cover**:
    *   Share distribution
    *   Decision-making processes
    *   Dividend policy
    *   Exit strategies (buy-sell provisions)
    *   Dispute resolution
*   **Format**: Notarized agreement

### 30. Director/Administrator Appointment Document
*   **Purpose**: Formal appointment of company directors
*   **Requirements**:
    *   **At least one Resident Administrator** (must reside in Andorra for tax residency/substance requirements)
    *   Board meeting minutes or shareholder resolution
    *   Acceptance of appointment
*   **Filed With**: Company Register

## **E. ONGOING COMPLIANCE DOCUMENTS (Post-Registration)**

### 31. Annual Accounts (Comptes Anuals)
*   **Purpose**: Audited financial statements
*   **Must Include**:
    *   Balance sheet
    *   Income statement
    *   Cash flow statement
    *   Notes to accounts
*   **Filing**: Annual submission to Commerce Register and Tax Authority

### 32. Corporate Tax Return (Impost sobre Societats)
*   **Purpose**: Annual tax filing for FundLab SL
*   **Tax Rate**: Andorra's corporate tax is **10%** (one of EU's lowest)
*   **Filing Deadline**: Typically within 3-6 months after fiscal year-end
*   **Authority**: Departament de Tributs i Fronteres

### 33. GDPR Compliance Records
*   **Purpose**: Ongoing documentation of data protection compliance
*   **Must Maintain**:
    *   Record of Processing Activities (ROPA)
    *   Data breach register (even if empty)
    *   User rights request logs (access, deletion, etc.)
*   **Review**: Annual review and updates

### 34. Board Meeting Minutes
*   **Purpose**: Document all board decisions
*   **Frequency**: As required by statutes (typically quarterly)
*   **Must Include**:
    *   Date, attendees, agenda
    *   Resolutions passed
    *   Voting results
*   **Storage**: Company records book

### 35. Shareholder Meeting Minutes (General Assembly)
*   **Purpose**: Document shareholder decisions (approving accounts, dividends, major decisions)
*   **Frequency**: Minimum annually (Annual General Meeting)
*   **Filed With**: Company Register (if required by Andorran law)

## **KEY NOTES**

1. **Minimum Capital**: €3,000 must be deposited before incorporation
2. **Foreign Investment Approval**: Required if non-Andorran shareholders own >10%
3. **Resident Administrator**: Mandatory for tax residency and economic substance
4. **Timeline**: Full registration process typically takes **2-4 months**
5. **Professional Advisors**: Strongly recommended:
    *   Andorran lawyer (for formation and foreign investment authorization)
    *   Gestoría/accounting firm (for tax compliance and ongoing filings)
    *   Data protection consultant (for GDPR compliance)
6. **Language**: Most official documents must be in **Catalan or Spanish**
7. **Costs**: Budget for:
    *   Notary fees (~€500-1,500)
    *   Commercial registration (~€200-500)
    *   Foreign investment application (~€1,000-3,000)
    *   Legal/advisory fees (~€3,000-10,000)
    *   Annual accounting/tax services (~€2,000-5,000/year)
